Buttered Toast

Description

Just like mama used to make. Butter on toast.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Toast
  • A buttering knife

Steps

  1. Begin by taking your butter out of the refrigerator 5 minutes before so it softens up a little.
  2. Toast your bread in the toaster.
  3. Gently use your butter knife to spread the softened butter on the toast.
