Sliced Apples

Description

It's apples cut into slices. What else can I say?

Ingredients

  • An apple
  • A sharp knife

Steps

  1. Carefully grip your apple by the top center.
  2. Using your knife, cut the apple from top to bottom, getting as close to the core as you can without including it.
  3. Repeat on the other three sides.
  4. Cut the larger wedges into consistent sizes.
